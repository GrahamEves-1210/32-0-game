Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

32-0 Game ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website 32-0game.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit or interact with our site.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account data: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name (username) to provide leaderboard and profile features.

Game data: We store your game results (score, wins, lineup, and championship status) to power your profile, leaderboard rankings, and badges.

Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized information about how visitors use the site, including pages visited, session duration, and general geographic region.

Device data: Standard browser information such as IP address, browser type, and operating system may be collected automatically by our analytics and advertising partners.

Cookies & Advertising

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and to serve personalized ads. Our advertising partners may use cookies to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.

We work with Google AdSense to display ads on this site. Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Monumetric Advertising

This Site is affiliated with Monumetric (dba for The Blogger Network, LLC) for the purposes of placing advertising on the Site, and Monumetric will collect and use certain data for advertising purposes. To learn more about Monumetric's data usage, click here: Publisher Advertising Privacy

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects data such as pages viewed, time on site, and general location. This data is aggregated and anonymized. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

• Operate and improve the 32-0 Game experience
• Display leaderboards, badges, and profile statistics
• Serve and optimize advertising
• Analyze site usage and fix issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (Supabase for database hosting, Google for analytics and advertising, Monumetric for advertising) solely to operate the site and serve ads.

Data Retention

Account and game data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
